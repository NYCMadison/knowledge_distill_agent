Food and Natural Resources
Business as usual is no longer an option in the way we produce, distribute, and consume our food.
Population growth, urbanisation, migration, resource scarcity, climate change and environmental degradation are creating urgent and complex challenges. Our food production system must become
fairer, more sustainable, and respectful to the environment
. And healthier.
Discover how EU research and innovation are exploring ways to reconciling what we eat with the needs of the planet.
